Fourier Series

About:

A Fourier series is a periodic function made up of the summation of various waves. You can use a Fourier series to approximate any wave. This is only approximating a square wave which are mostly used in digital circuits as a clock.

You can see as you increase N, the sin wave more closely resembles a square wave.
