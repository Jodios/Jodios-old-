Bubble Sort Sort

Description:

Bubble sort is a sorting algorithm that iterates through a list, compares adjacent elements and swaps them if they're in the wrong order.

Complexity:

Bubble sort's worst case scenario is O(n2), meaning that at its worst, it will make n2 comparisons. It's the simplest sorting algorithm, and also one of the most inefficient ones.
